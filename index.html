By using natural cosmetics you are not only benefiting your body but doing something good for the environment too! The products that we have curated are free from harmful chemical cocktails and microplastics and contain high-quality organic oils and raw plant materials that are largely organically grown.

We follow exceptionally strict guidelines when it comes to reviewing a brand - without compromise. A brand is not added to our selection if it does not fulfil the natural cosmetics standards, no matter how high in demand it may be. Occasionally, the composition or formulas of individual products are modified. In such cases, the products are reviewed once more to ensure that natural cosmetic standards are still met.

We are very cautious when it comes to brands that develop both conventional as well as natural products. At Ecco Verde, such brands are far and few. If so, we only list the products that are truly natural. That being said, we do lean toward forgoing the acquisition of brands that offer such a product catalogue.
